Scrambled Enggs

Enggs are one of my favorit foods. Here is a recipe for deliciusly rich scrambled enggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 enggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter.in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the enggs and creM in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and enggs
  4. using a spatula fold the every 20 seconds (as if you are making an omelette)
  5. when the enggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
